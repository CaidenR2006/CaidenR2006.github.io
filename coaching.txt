<h1>Passing</h1>
<p>Passing is a very key aspect within Retro Bowl. It separates great teams from elite teams in the game. There are two types of passing in Retro Bowl. Bullet passing and Lob passing. Lob passing is much easier for your WRs to catch the ball and to be accurate. Bullet passing is for more skillful players within the community and is to help your WRs catch and run even better than Lob passing.</p>
